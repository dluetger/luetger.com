NASA divides the work of building its new spacecraft among dozens of teams. They're constantly exchanging documentation and hardware as they design and build. If information and parts donâ€™t arrive on time, the entire launch could get delayed. A small team is in charge of tracking these exchanges. We designed and built a system for them to track deadlines, points of contact, and the status of each asset. We saved them several hours per week by automating their weekly reports. We also gave them a single, authoritative place to keep their critical mission data. This is crucial for maintaining accuracy. By introducing efficiency wherever we can, we help push our mission forward.
